[ What We Offer ]

Comprehensive Shop Drawings
for Every Stage of Construction

At El-Marwa Construction Designs, we deliver detailed and precise shop drawings that guide your construction projects from the initial stages to successful completion. Our services ensure seamless execution, optimized resource utilization, and adherence to the highest engineering standards.

  • Precision: Detailed representations of structural elements with unparalleled accuracy.
  • Efficiency: Streamlined workflows and optimized material usage to reduce waste.
  • Compliance: Adherence to international codes and standards for flawless execution.
🚜

Comprehensive Excavation and Soil Preparation Plans

We provide detailed shop drawings that cover every aspect of soil reports and excavation requirements, ensuring a solid foundation for your project.

  • Cross-sections detailing excavation depths and lateral support techniques.
  • Safety precautions and methodologies for sidewall stabilization.
  • Detailed plans for soil replacement (if necessary), highlighting compacted layers and approved materials.

Case Study: For a large industrial project, we prepared detailed excavation and soil stabilization plans, reducing the risk of settlement issues and ensuring compliance with local regulations.

🏗️

Structural Shop Drawings for Foundations and Concrete Elements

Our shop drawings include precise details for all structural elements, ensuring accurate execution of the foundation and reinforced concrete works.

  • Detailed sections for normal and reinforced concrete, specifying grades, dimensions, and elevations.
  • Horizontal, vertical, and side sections to illustrate the complete structural system.
  • Additional sectional views for complex structural elements, enabling clarity and accuracy.
🔧

Reinforcement Detailing with BBS (Bar Bending Schedule)

We specialize in creating comprehensive reinforcement detailing, ensuring maximum efficiency and minimal material wastage.

  • Main and additional reinforcement details for each structural element.
  • BBS (Bar Bending Schedule) with clear specifications for bar diameters, lengths, overlaps, and cut lengths.
  • A systemized approach to minimize steel wastage while optimizing project costs.

Case Study: Our team provided reinforcement detailing for a high-rise residential building, reducing steel wastage by 12% and saving significant project costs.

🔌

MEP Coordination and Shop Drawings

Our MEP shop drawings ensure perfect integration of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing systems, eliminating conflicts during construction.

  • Layouts for HVAC, plumbing, and electrical systems optimized for space and efficiency.
  • Clash detection and resolution using advanced software like Revit and BIM.
  • Coordination between structural and architectural designs for a seamless construction process.

Case Study: For a commercial building, we identified and resolved over 40 potential clashes in the MEP layout, reducing project delays by 15 days.

⚙️

Structural Steel Fabrication Drawings

We create accurate steel fabrication shop drawings that provide clear guidance to fabricators, ensuring efficiency and precision.

  • Detailed assembly plans with welding, bolting, and cutting details.
  • Optimized material usage to minimize costs and waste.
  • Compliance with international safety and fabrication standards.
📐

Architectural Shop Drawings

Our architectural shop drawings bring every design detail to life, ensuring a clear understanding of project elements.

  • Floor plans, elevations, and sectional details with precise measurements.
  • Joinery and fixture detailing for interior spaces.
  • Integration of architectural elements with structural and MEP systems for a cohesive project design.
