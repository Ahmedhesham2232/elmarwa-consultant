Coastal International Road Support System Enhancement

At El-Marwa Construction Designs, we are dedicated to delivering cutting-edge engineering solutions that uphold the highest standards of safety, efficiency, and sustainability in large-scale infrastructure projects. As part of our unwavering commitment to excellence, we have successfully enhanced the support system for excavation works along the Coastal International Road, reinforcing its structural integrity and ensuring a seamless construction process.

Through meticulous planning and innovative engineering techniques, we expanded the road’s support structure, safeguarded critical underground pipelines, and implemented advanced stabilization measures—ensuring long-term durability and minimal disruption. Below, we outline the key modifications and strategic enhancements that made this project a success:

Our Role in the Project

📌 Scope of Work & Key Enhancements

  • 🚧 Expansion of the Retaining System: To reinforce the Coastal International Road, we extended the retaining system from 2.5 meters to 4 meters, significantly enhancing the stability of the excavation area. This upgrade ensured safer execution of construction activities by:
    • 🛡️ Preventing soil displacement and structural shifts.
    • 🏗️ Securing the integrity of the road’s foundation.
    • 🚦 Maintaining uninterrupted traffic flow during construction.
  • ⚠️ Protection of Critical Utility Lines: One of the major engineering challenges was addressing existing underground petroleum and gas pipelines along the project path. Our expert team successfully identified, secured, and safeguarded these essential infrastructure components:
    • 🛢️ Three petroleum pipelines (8”, 12”, and 16” diameters) – owned by the Petroleum Pipelines Company.
    • 🔥 A 30” high-pressure gas pipeline – managed by GASCO.
    To mitigate risks, we implemented 🛠️ advanced protection measures, ensuring construction proceeded safely without compromising the integrity of these vital assets.

Engineering Solutions & Execution Strategy

  • 📊 Comprehensive Structural Assessments: Conducted in-depth engineering studies to analyze soil stability and optimize the support system for enhanced safety and durability.
  • 🛡️ State-of-the-Art Protection Methods: Implemented cutting-edge reinforcement techniques to safeguard underground pipelines, ensuring zero risk of damage or operational disruption.
  • ⚙️ Precision Excavation Techniques: Utilized high-precision excavation methods to navigate complex underground infrastructure while strictly adhering to international safety standards.
  • 🔗 Seamless Integration with Existing Infrastructure: Ensured the modified retaining system was seamlessly integrated with the existing road and utility networks, maintaining structural integrity and operational efficiency.

Project Impact & Achievements

  • 🏗️ Enhanced Safety: Expanding the retaining system depth significantly improved site stability, reducing excavation risks and ensuring a safer work environment for all site operations.
  • ⚙️ Infrastructure Longevity: By reinforcing pipeline structures, we minimized future risks of operational disruptions, ensuring long-term reliability and durability.
  • 📉 Cost & Time Efficiency: Our proactive risk mitigation strategies eliminated costly delays and optimized project execution, leading to seamless progress and on-time delivery.

Engineering Excellence in Action
